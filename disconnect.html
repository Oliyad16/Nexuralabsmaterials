Disconnect Nexura Labs Materials

This page explains how users can disconnect accounting access from Nexura Labs Materials.

Disconnect Instructions

  1. Open your accounting provider account settings.
  2. Go to connected apps or app integrations.
  3. Select Nexura Labs Materials.
  4. Choose disconnect or revoke access.

After disconnecting, Nexura Labs Materials will no longer be authorized to access connected accounting data. Existing procurement or invoice records may be retained as required for business, audit, tax, and compliance purposes.
