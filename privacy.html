This Privacy Policy explains how Nexura Labs Materials LLC ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit nexuralabsmaterials.com or make a purchase.

Payments are handled by our payment processor (including QuickBooks Payments / Intuit). Your payment information is subject to that processor's privacy and security practices in addition to ours.

We use cookies and similar technologies for site functionality, analytics, and security. You can control cookies through your browser settings; some features may not work without them.

6. Data retention.

We retain personal information as long as needed to fulfill the purposes described here, including legal, accounting, and tax requirements.

7. Security.

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect personal information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

8. Your choices.

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, subject to legal limits, by contacting us. You may opt out of marketing emails via the unsubscribe link.

Our site is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their information.

10. Changes.
